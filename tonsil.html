Tonsillitis

Also called: tonsil inflammation.
An inflammation of the two oval-shaped pads of tissue at the back of the throat.

Requires a medical diagnosis
Symptoms include sore throat, difficulty swallowing and tender lymph nodes.

People may experience:
Pain areas: in the ear
Pain circumstances: can occur while swallowing
Whole body: chills, fatigue, fever, or malaise
Nasal: congestion or runny nose
Speech: laryngitis or impaired voice
Also common: enlarged neck lymph nodes, sore throat, swollen tonsils, bad breath, excess salivation, mouth breathing, poor appetite, pus, snoring, or tender lymph nodes
