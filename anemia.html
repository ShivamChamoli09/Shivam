Anemia

Also called: lack of blood.
A condition in which the blood doesn't have enough healthy red blood cells.

Requires a medical diagnosis
Symptoms may include fatigue, skin pallor, shortness of breath, light-headedness, dizziness or a fast heartbeat.

People may experience:
Whole body: dizziness, fatigue, light-headedness, malaise, or weakness
Heart: fast heart rate or palpitations
Also common: brittle nails, headache, pallor, or shortness of breath
